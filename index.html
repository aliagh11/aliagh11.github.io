Ali Aghadadashi

Founder • Researcher • Engineer

Education: M.Sc. Energy Systems Engineering
Institute: Sharif University of Technology

I specialize in optimization and artificial intelligence for energy and thermal systems, with a strong background in climate change modeling and renewable energy integration. My research focuses on intelligent optimization frameworks and decision-support tools that enhance the efficiency and sustainability of complex energy infrastructures.

As an avid programmer, I use Python, Java, and C to design and implement analytical models, optimization algorithms, simulations, and engineering software. I developed a dedicated software tool to optimize the thermodynamics of refrigeration and freezer cycles, and I also 3D-designed and manufactured hundreds of units across four cutting-edge commercial freezers and refrigerators — see the Industrial Working Experience section.
